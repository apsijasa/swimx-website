Our Services

We use cutting-edge technology, like the eo SwimBETTER device, to deliver detailed, accurate analyses that will revolutionize your swimming technique.

Stroke Force Analysis

We measure the power in each stroke, pinpointing asymmetries and improvement areas to maximize your water propulsion.

Force Distribution Analysis

We evaluate how you distribute force throughout your stroke cycle, optimizing energy use and reducing unnecessary effort.

Stroke Pressure Analysis

We study pressure points during your stroke to enhance water grip and maximize propulsion at every phase.

Efficiency Analysis by Stroke

We examine techniques for each swimming style, offering personalized improvements for freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly.

Competition Analysis

We assess your performance under competitive conditions, providing targeted strategies to improve your race times.

Force vs. Time Analysis

We analyze stroke force throughout the entire motion—down to the millisecond—with video feedback for even clearer insights.

Detailed Evaluation Process

Discover step by step how we conduct our advanced technical analysis to enhance your swimming performance.

Initial Assessment

We start with a personal interview to understand your goals, athletic background, and any injuries or limitations, and we record your current basic metrics.

Data Capture

Using the eo SwimBETTER device, we record precise data on your stroke mechanics, water pressure, and movement patterns.

Biomechanical Analysis

We analyze all the collected data to identify patterns, imbalances, and opportunities for technique improvement.

Personalized Report

We create a detailed report with clear visualizations, benchmarks, and specific recommendations to enhance your technique—all paired with video feedback.

Review Session

Depending on your plan, we hold a consultation session to go over the results, answer questions, and outline your personalized action plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

We answer the most common questions about our advanced technical analysis service.

How long does a complete analysis session last?

A complete session lasts about 60 to 90 minutes, including equipment setup, roughly 30 minutes of in-water data capture, and an initial review. The detailed report is provided afterward, with the consultation session scheduled separately based on your plan.

Do I need to be an advanced swimmer to benefit from the analysis?

Not at all. Our analysis benefits swimmers of all levels. Beginners and intermediates get essential insights to quickly improve technique and avoid bad habits, while advanced swimmers receive fine-tuned recommendations for competitive edge.

How do I interpret the report I receive?

Our reports are designed to be user-friendly—even if you aren’t tech-savvy. They include clear visuals, benchmark comparisons, and specific recommendations. For Standard and Premium plans, a consultation session will walk you through everything.

How often should I have an analysis?

The ideal frequency depends on your goals and level. Most swimmers benefit from an initial analysis followed by a reassessment in 2 months, while competitive swimmers may require monthly evaluations for continuous adjustments.

Does the technology work for all swimming styles?

Yes, our technology is designed to analyze the four main swimming styles—freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly—as well as transitions and starts for competitive swimmers and triathletes.

What should I bring to the analysis session?

Just bring your regular swimming gear—swimsuit, goggles, and cap. We recommend using the suit you typically wear in training or competition so that the analysis reflects your normal technique. We provide all the necessary technical equipment, and you might want to bring a towel and dry clothes for after the session.
